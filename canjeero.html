Canjeelo

Description

Canjeelo, a cherished Somali pancake, encapsulates the essence of simplicity and flavor. Crafted from basic ingredients which is flour, baking powder, salt, and water. Canjeelo's batter is allowed to rest, producing tender yet slightly crispy pancakes

Cooked on a griddle, each Canjeelo carries a delicate aroma and a round, golden-brown charm. Served with an array of toppings, from honey to butter, it transforms into a versatile canvas for tastes. Whether enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or a delightful snack, Canjeelo is a testament to Somali tradition, offering a taste of cultural heritage in every delicious bite.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Oil(for cooking)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Batter

    In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix these dry ingredients well.


  2. Add Water

    Gradually add water while stirring the dry ingredients. Start with about 2 cups of water and mix until you achieve a smooth and pourable batter. You may need to add a bit more water to achieve the right consistency.

  3. Rest the Batter

    Let the batter rest for about 15-20 minutes. This allows the flour to absorb the water and results in a softer texture.
  4. Heat the Pan

    Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of oil to lightly coat the pan.
  5. Cooking the Pancakes

    Give the batter a good stir before each pancake to ensure even consistency.

    Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the heated pan. Quickly spread it out into a thin, round shape using the back of the ladle.
  6. Cook Both Sides

    Cook the Canjeelo for about 1-2 minutes on one side until you see bubbles forming on the surface and the edges appear cooked.

    Flip the pancake and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes until it's golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Repeat the Process

    Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding a bit of oil to the pan before cooking each new pancake.
